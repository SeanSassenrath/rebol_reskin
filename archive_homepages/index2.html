Most software systems have become needlessly complex.

We rebel against that complexity, fighting it with the most powerful tool available, language itself.

Viva la Rebolution

REBOL is not a traditional computer language. It's not for everyone. It's for thinkers and doers -- those who want more power and flexibility in their language, and those who want to solve computing problems in smarter more elegant ways.

Loose the GB's

Really hammer in the idea that Rebol does amazing, powerful things at a fraction of the space / processing power. This is the idea that makes Rebol so great. Build complex ideas simply and without huge file sizes.
